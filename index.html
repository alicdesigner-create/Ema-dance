About AMA

Grow & Glow
With AMA

AMA Dance Club is more than a studio — it's a community built around confidence, expression, and transformation through dance.

What started in Chicago has grown into an evolving experience now expanding in Denver. Rooted in the Anna Musia Heels Method, AMA blends strong technique with embodiment and musicality, helping you not just learn movement, but truly understand and own it. We offer a variety of dance styles and levels designed to meet you where you are and support your growth.

Whether you're a beginner or experienced dancer, this is a space to grow, connect, and step into your full presence.

Build confidence, master your movement, and connect with a community that supports your growth.

Founder & Teacher

Anna
Musia

Anna Musia is the creator of the Anna Musia Heels Method — a refined approach to dance that blends technical precision, musicality, and deep embodiment. With an international teaching background, Anna has taught in multiple countries, working with dancers of all levels and helping them unlock both their skill and their confidence.

Over 500 students have trained with Anna, many of whom have gone on to become confident performers and successful dance teachers themselves. Her work goes beyond choreography — it's about understanding your body, mastering weight transfer and control, and developing a presence that feels natural, powerful, and fully expressed.

Anna's classes are designed to give you real, tangible progress. You don't just learn steps — you learn how to move with intention, expand your range, and build a strong technical foundation that supports your individuality. At the same time, her method guides you into a deeper connection with yourself, helping you feel more confident not only in dance, but in the way you carry yourself in everyday life.

This is where technique meets transformation — and where dancers step into their next level.
